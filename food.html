JAPAN

Food
page2 (current) page3 page4 page5

Japan can be the country that need no introduction when it comes to food. The Japanese value their food as much as they value their well-being; everyone at every island, every province, and every city are dedicated to improve their cooking skils to make what ever is trending either in their house, neighboorhood or the entire city. Food festivals pop up in every region where everyone can show to the people how they have improved on making whatever food they enjoy eating whether it would be Sushi, Mochi, Dumplings, Pasta, Ramen, Bentos, Takoyaki, there is at least one thing out there for everyone. There are even entire festivals build towards cooking foods for poeple.
